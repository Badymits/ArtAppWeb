Background of the website

With the advent of the internet becoming somewhat of an integral part of our modern society, it came with the benefit of bridging the world together. As such, people became more and more accepting of what were once stigmatized. However, despite the modern society being more accepting now compared to the past, certain minority groups and communities are still faced with the struggle of not being welcomed with open arms. One of these being the LGBTQ+ community. A community that is still shamed to this day for simply being who they are.



Through the art gallery, Coming Out, the artists hope to celebrate the LGBTQ+ community for their bravery and resilience, as well as raise awareness to the struggles that those part of the community have experienced.
