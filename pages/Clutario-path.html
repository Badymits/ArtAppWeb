Path

Djuliana Mae Clutario

Not every queer individual finds a support once they come out, therefore they hold secrets that they are afraid to share. Such as fear of being rejected, isolation or ridiculed and that's how I portray the darkness of this painting, that they are trapped and lost at themselves.

As for the light, it represents the “Coming out” It is a very dauntless act and a difficult process to take in for each individual but it will be rewarding in the end because you may feel free and can be finally true to who you are.
