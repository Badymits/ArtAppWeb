Araw-araw

Elijah Oshea Garcia

Araw-araw is an artwork depicting the concept of “us against the world” for a couple who is part of the LGBTQ+ Community. The line “Sa mundong puno ng pagbabawal, ikaw parin ang pipiliin ko sa araw-araw” showcases how despite the world being conservative and limiting, the LGBTQ+ Community will keep on fighting for what they love.
