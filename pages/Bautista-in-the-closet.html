In The Closet

Aleck Bautista

In The Closet is a visualization of how it feels like to not be able to come out as your preferred gender. I personally have never gone through something like this but this is how I would imagine it being like. It is about a little girl who is too afraid to come out to her parents and friends so she just stays in the closet feeling lonely and thinking that nobody could ever imagine what it is like being in her situation.
