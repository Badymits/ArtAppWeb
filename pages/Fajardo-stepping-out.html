Stepping Out

Shaer Kaira Antoinette Fajardo

Being queer myself, I know how hard it was to show who I was in the world. Because of my fear of being rejected by the people I love, I kept myself in the closet for a very long time. What I did not realize in all those years was that I had much more positivity to offer than the negativity I would face.

In this artwork, a transgender man can be seen hiding in the closet. He is scared of the idea of getting out of there because of all the spikes that will strike him before he even steps a foot, but reaches out anyway. To his pleasant surprise, one of the spikes melts at the presence of his hand.
