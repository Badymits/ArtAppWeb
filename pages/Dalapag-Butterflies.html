Butterflies

Glendy Dalapag

They say when you meet someone special, you feel butterflies in your stomach. And those butterflies for me, know no gender. As the saying goes “Love is love.” And love, is a beautiful thing that brings color to our lives. It loves who it chooses to be with. Those butterflies won’t stay hidden. They will sworm in your stomach till you come out with pride, and that will be a beautiful phenomenon.
