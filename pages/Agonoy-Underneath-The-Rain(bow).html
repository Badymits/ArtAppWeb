Underneath The Rain(bow)

Ralp kemuel Agonoy

The artwork I made contains a lot of symbolisms and a hidden text of this character’s true identity. This explains the rainbow in the background being correlated to the LGBTQ. I gave this person that kind of expression, because this person does not care about other people’s opinions - the opinions of people are the rain.

To add context as to why I chose the opinions of people to be the rain, is because I saw a lot of people barging in people’s personal spaces/beliefs. Lastly, the umbrella is “someone” he holds dear, and that person is the one that keeps him from harm. I am not an ally nor an enemy; I am just here to do good to everyone.
