Boy in the Closet

Arriz Camille Dahan

This is about a boy who is afraid of coming out of the closet. Despite the warm world outside, he is simply not yet ready and would rather stay in the dark, and cold closet. To give himself comfort, he keeps his favorite companion with him, his favorite doll. The title of the online exhibit is Coming Out, but I decided to show a simple story that still happens in the present.

Either because of other people or just themselves, there are people who are not yet comfortable about coming out of their own closets. I hope that this artwork, in some way, gives those people comfort and reassurance. That it is perfectly fine to take your time in getting to know yourself. Every one of us goes through our lives at our own pace.
