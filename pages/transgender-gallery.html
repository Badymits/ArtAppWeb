Transgender



Transgender is an umbrella term that encompasses all people who experience and identify with a different gender than that which their assigned sex at birth would suggest.

-An umbrella term that can also describe someone who identifies as a gender other than a woman or man, such as non-binary, genderqueer, genderfluid, no gender, multiple genders, or other gender identities.

However, it's important to note that not everyone uses it to describe themselves.
