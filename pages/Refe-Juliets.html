Juliets

Noel Gabriel Refe

The artwork “Juliets” was made by Noel Gabriel V Refe, Juliets is about 2 young girls, and it is heavily influenced by William Shakespeare’s Romeo and Juliet but instead of their families that have conflict, their conflict is their sexuality. And then time came and they give in to their feelings and gave each other a chance.
