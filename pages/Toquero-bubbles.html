Bubbles

Jean Mizpah Toquero

A happy couple playing with bubbles in a bathtub. I made this artwork after being inspired by the series “Heartstopper”. Coming out takes time, it is a very difficult process, and one day you will accept your sexual orientation or gender identity and share that to the people you care about, and it will reflect on you.

Here shows a boy who became a part of the LGBTQ+ community, who has someone that waited and loved him along the way. As you can see I added a rainbow reflection that shines on him. The hands holding his face shows the comfort and love he gives him, and that he’s proud of him.
