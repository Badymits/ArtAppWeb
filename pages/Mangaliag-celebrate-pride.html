Celebrate Pride

Annika Lei Mangaliag

Celebrate Pride is an artwork that highlights people’s different experiences of gender and gender expression. The artwork has the goal of showcasing our differences, may they be internal or external, in a positive and uplifting way. I wanted to dismantle existing generalizations formed by society pertaining to gender.

Instead, this illustration is rooted in gender neutrality. Regardless of how people choose to express their individuality, it shouldn’t need to cater to anyone else’s idea of gender besides their own. Moreso, this artwork is a visualization of where our community is hopefully headed—a space where all people, especially the LGBTQ+ community, have the freedom to express who they are
