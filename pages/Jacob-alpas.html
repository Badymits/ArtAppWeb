When we discover how many spiteful people there are in the world, cruelty rises to the surface. Homophobia is a trait they consider commonplace, and they pour venom against anyone who belongs to the LGBTQ community. Regardless of the reality that they treat the LGBTQ community like a monster and an outcast of society, we are all equal by nature and my artwork illustrates the harsh reality of their mistreatment. Unfortunately, such behavior simply exists to make a person doubt their entire existence as someone who is perceived as being different by society.
