Reflection

Marion Christopher Fabian

The artwork shows an anthropomorphic cat in a bedroom looking at themselves in a mirror, but instead of their reflection, it’s a depiction of what they find to be a self that they find themselves happy with. Being that everything around the setting is of a monochromatic nature while the only one in color is the reflection, one can infer that the cat prefers to be the one that they see in the reflection.

I based this artwork off of my experiences of gender dysphoria. This idea is based off of several drawings I’ve made in the past. This specific iteration is based off of a drawing I found from 2019 hidden within my sketchbooks. Given our theme for this project, I decided to bring this piece justice by digitizing and coloring it.

With the only words written on the art piece being “Soon, I will be.”, I hope people would find the courage to accept themselves as who they are and what they want to become, even if the going gets tough. Knowing that the journey to self-acceptance isn’t a smooth road, I just want to remind people going down that road to know that “Soon, you will be.” Be whatever you want to be.
