Big Fan of Space

Samantha Aguirre

Being an aroace person myself, I'm a big fan of space, both outer and personal. Jokes aside, this does not mean I and other aroaces despise connection with other people, nor will we magically change after finding "the one" or being told of the many phrases written in the artwork. We may not experience romantic or sexual attraction, but there are so many other ways to love people and develop meaningful relationships.
