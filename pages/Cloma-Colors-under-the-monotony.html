Colors Under the Monotony

Jerik Cloma

With “Colors Under the Monotony”, I wanted to show what lies underneath the everyday identities of people we see on our daily commute through the colors I chose, which come from different pride flags (Bisexual, Pansexual, and Transgender pride flags respectively). The setting also represents their journeys.
