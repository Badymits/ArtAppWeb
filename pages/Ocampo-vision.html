Vision

David Rafael Ocampo

The painting that I created is about “vision”. Vision is like a sight where us people have different kinds of roots, as you can see in the painting there are different kinds of shapes or so called “roots'', they’re somehow connected to each other. The blue color or the center one is the main source of the roots, it represents the main source of tranquility.

Here are the colors that I used in the painting: 1. Burnt Sienna 2. Burnt Umber 3. Purple Red 4. Titanium White 5. Viridian 6. Scarlet 7. Phthalo Blue 8. Raw Senna.
